Your mission

Train like operators in the field — without leaving dry land.

Light inspection-class ROVs are the workhorses of underwater infrastructure: small enough for tight spaces, stable enough for precision surveys, and tethered for unlimited runtime. Inspect-Me puts you in the pilot seat with the same control paradigm used on real compact inspection vehicles.

  • Navigate harbors, hulls, and submerged structures
  • Hold attitude while maneuvering in current and drag
  • Operate pan/tilt FPV with a live secondary video feed
  • Remap every axis to your gamepad or keyboard

Built for realism

Everything you need to feel the water.

Inspired by professional inspection workflows — not arcade flight. Every system is tuned for believable ROV handling in shallow and moderate depths.

01

4-DOF piloting

Dual-stick control: surge and yaw on one axis pair, heave and sway on the other. Vehicle stays level — just like typical inspection ROV defaults.

02

Marine hydrodynamics

Drag, buoyancy, and restoring forces modeled after marine vehicle dynamics — so momentum, drift, and station-keeping feel authentic.

03

FPV camera tilt

Look up, down, and around structures with smooth camera pitch — essential for hull scans, pipe runs, and confined-space navigation.

04

Live video stream

Broadcast your FPV feed over HTTP as MJPEG — mirror real operations where pilots and observers watch separate displays.

05

Full input remapping

Bind surge, sway, heave, yaw, and camera tilt to any gamepad or keyboard. Swap devices mid-session with live detection.

06

Underwater atmosphere

Caustics, murk, and depth cues create an immersive training environment — from open harbor water to tight inspection zones.

Light inspection class

The ROV category you're training on.

No brand names — just the vehicle class used worldwide for visual inspection in ports, dams, aquaculture, and industrial assets.

Typical profile

  • Size Compact — often under 1 m, tens of kg in air
  • Depth rating Shallow to moderate (commonly 100–300 m class)
  • Propulsion 4–5 thrusters — horizontal, vertical, lateral
  • Power Surface-supplied via tether umbilical

Real-world missions

  • Port, marina & harbor infrastructure surveys
  • Vessel hull, propeller & rudder inspection
  • Dam, lock & hydroelectric structure checks
  • Pipeline, cable & aquaculture monitoring
  • Confined-space search & object verification

Why sim training matters

  • Build muscle memory before expensive field time
  • Practice precision in current and limited visibility
  • Learn dual-display workflows (pilot + observer)
  • Fail safely — recover from collisions and drift

FAQ

Common questions

When will it be available?

Inspect-Me is in active development. Star or watch the GitHub repo to get notified when the first public build drops.

Is this tied to a specific ROV manufacturer?

No. Inspect-Me models the light inspection-class category — compact tethered vehicles with 4-DOF piloting and FPV workflows common across the industry.

Can observers watch the FPV feed separately?

Yes — the sim will stream MJPEG over HTTP on a local port, so pilots and observers can use separate displays, just like real operations.

What hardware will I need?

A PC with a dedicated GPU is recommended. Keyboard, mouse, and gamepad/joystick support are built in, with full remappable bindings.
